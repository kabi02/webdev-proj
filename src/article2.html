Vigan

by TravelMates




Are you looking for a destination that offers a perfect mix of history, culture, and natural beauty? Look no further than Vigan, a city located in the province of Ilocos Sur, Philippines. Vigan is known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture, delicious cuisine, and rich heritage. In this ultimate travel guide, we'll uncover the best of Vigan and why it should be on every traveler's bucket list.

Credits: Flikr - Jaya / Creative Commons

One of the main reasons why Vigan is such a popular tourist spot is its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture. The city's cobblestone streets and centuries-old buildings transport you back in time, providing a glimpse into what life was like in the Philippines during the Spanish colonial period. The best way to explore Vigan's historic charm is by taking a stroll down Calle Crisologo, a street lined with ancestral houses and old-fashioned lamps. You can also visit the Syquia Mansion Museum, a restored 19th-century house that was once the residence of former Philippine President Elpidio Quirino.

Credits: @crisibanez, @whatsin.jansplate, @lutonikuyalevi on Instagram

Vigan is also known for its mouth-watering cuisine. Some of the must-try dishes include Vigan longganisa, a sweet and garlicky sausage, and empanada, a crispy pastry filled with meat, vegetables, and egg. You can find these dishes in almost every corner of Vigan, but the best places to try them are the food stalls in Plaza Burgos and the Vigan Public Market.

Credits: Marky Ramone Go from nomadicexperiences.com

Vigan has a rich and diverse cultural heritage that reflects its unique history. The city is home to many cultural landmarks and museums, including the Bantay Bell Tower, which dates back to the 16th century and offers panoramic views of the city, and the Vigan Cathedral, a beautiful Baroque-style church that was built in the 18th century. You can also visit the National Museum Ilocos Region Complex, which showcases the region's history and culture through various exhibits.

Vigan is a hidden gem in the Philippines that offers a unique and unforgettable travel experience. With its historic charm, delicious cuisine, rich heritage, and stunning natural beauty, Vigan is a must-visit destination for any traveler.
